Preparing my Heart

"Comfort, comfort, my people" says your God."

Devotionals

In order to help prepare our hearts and minds for the spring break mission trip(s), and to better understand God's love for the people that we want to serve and understand better, Sacred Road Ministries has us do devotionals. I wanted to briefly discuss how helpful these Biblical devotionals are in the week prior and the week during the trip.

Fair warning, I'm a Christian, and so I'll be talking about my faith in God, Jesus Christ, the Holy Spirit, and how I've seen God at work in my life. I'm not trying to convince you of anything, and I don't want to make you uncomfortable. I'm am also not trying to single out a particular ministry that is caring for Native people, because there are several people, both native and not, who are walking toward reconciliation between native and mainstream america, and who have a heart for seeing the native church become a part of the church as a whole. I'm simply talking about my personal experience and understanding. I'm not claiming to be wise or knowledgable, but I do want to share what I've experienced.

I'll be honest, in that most days, especially those before spring break, my mind is scattered, and I'm quite weak in body and spirit.
